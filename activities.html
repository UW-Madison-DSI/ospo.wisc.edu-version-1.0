Activities

The University of Wisconsin Open Source Program Office will be engaging in a variety of servies and activities to help you to better understand Open Source and to make better use of Open Source methodologies.

For Open Source Users

  • Symposia

    We will be hosting a symposium and training event aimed at growing open source projects and communities.

  • Workshops

    Will host a series of workshops with the Office of Business Engagement, the Division of Extension, and the campus open source ecosystem.

  • Surveys

    The UW OSPO will be conducting a campus-wide open source survey to get a picture of the campus culture and create an initial index of open source efforts and projects.

For Open Source Developers

  • Internships

    We will expanding our existing Open Source Internship program and broaden participation through a partnership with Madison College.

  • Training

    We will be conducting Carpentries-style training programs to hone the skills needed to be an effective open source contributor.
